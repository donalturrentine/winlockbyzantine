Winlock Byzantine Outreach

Winlock Byzantine Eastern Catholic Outreach is an official Outreach of the Holy Protection of Mary Byzantine Catholic Eparchy of Phoenix.

Catholics of all backgrounds from Cowlitz, Lewis, and Thurston Counties (in Southwest Washington State) attend Divine Liturgy “Eastern Catholic Mass” on Sundays at 1 pm in Winlock, Washington.

Located at Historic Sacred Heart Catholic Church:

216 NW Arden, Winlock, Washington

Regular Schedule

Sunday 1 pm * Every Sunday Divine Liturgy (fulfills Catholic obligation for Sunday Mass) 1 pm + Coffee and Social Hour to follow

Thursday 7 pm * Compline (Liturgy of the Hours Night Prayer)

Men’s Group

Meets every Wednesday for prayer, work projects, spiritual study, and fellowship.

Women’s Group

Regular meetings for prayer, projects, spiritual formation, and fellowship.

Join Us to Celebrate the Divine Liturgy

Winlock Byzantine Outreach welcomes Catholics of all traditions in Southwest Washington, fostering a community rooted in growing in the spiritual life together, through shared prayer and worship.
